ROBOTICS COMPONENTS – TECHNICAL SUMMARY

JUMPER CABLES
Purpose: Provide electrical connection between Arduino, sensors, modules.
Types:
- Male–Male
- Male–Female
- Female–Female

ON/OFF SWITCH
Function: Simple HIGH/LOW digital control by opening/closing the circuit.

5V WATER PUMP
Description: Small DC pump used for irrigation and cooling. Driven by 5V DC motor.

BATTERY TYPES
9V Battery: Six 1.5V cells in series.
AA Battery: Standard medium-size cell for consumer electronics.
AAA Battery: Smaller variant for compact devices.

BREADBOARD
Function: Solderless prototyping board. Vertical connection columns, horizontal power rails.

5V RELAY MODULE
Purpose: Control high‑voltage devices using Arduino.
Works by electromagnetic switching.

ADJUSTABLE POTENTIOMETER
Usage: Variable resistance control (brightness, volume, analog sensitivity).
