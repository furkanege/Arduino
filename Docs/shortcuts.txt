KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS

• Parentheses (): Press SHIFT + 8.
• Curly braces {}: Press ALT GR + 7.
• ALL CAPS: Press CAPSLOCK (light should be ON).
• All lowercase: Press CAPSLOCK again (light OFF).
• Single capital letter: Press SHIFT + letter.
• Semicolon (;): Press SHIFT + comma key.
• Less-than (<): Left of SHIFT.
• Greater-than (>): SHIFT + < key.
• Before uploading Arduino code: Always select correct BOARD and PORT.
